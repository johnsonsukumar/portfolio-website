Customer Segmentation Analysis

Used SQL and Excel to segment customers by behaviour, RFM score, and lifecycle stage — enabling targeted email and paid media campaigns that significantly improved engagement and revenue per customer.

ToolsSQL, Excel
TypeData Analysis
Result+35% Email Revenue
🔍

🎯 The Problem

The client was sending the same marketing emails to their entire database of 50,000+ customers — resulting in high unsubscribe rates, low click-through, and wasted ad spend on poorly targeted paid campaigns.

There was no customer segmentation in place. Everyone received the same message regardless of their purchase history, recency, or lifetime value.

🔧 Tools Used

SQL (BigQuery) Excel Google Sheets Looker Studio Mailchimp

🛠️ Approach

  • Extracted 12 months of order and customer data from the database using SQL
  • Built an RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) scoring model to rank every customer
  • Segmented customers into 5 groups: Champions, Loyal, At Risk, Hibernating, and Lost
  • Exported segments to Excel for further analysis and visualisation
  • Worked with the email team to create tailored messaging for each segment
  • Built a tracking dashboard in Looker Studio to monitor segment performance over time

💻 SQL Snippet — RFM Scoring

-- RFM Segmentation Query
WITH rfm_base AS (
  SELECT
    customer_id,
    DATE_DIFF(CURRENT_DATE(), MAX(order_date), DAY) AS recency,
    COUNT(order_id)                                    AS frequency,
    SUM(order_value)                                   AS monetary
  FROM orders
  WHERE order_date >= DATE_SUB(CURRENT_DATE(), INTERVAL 365 DAY)
  GROUP BY customer_id
),
rfm_scored AS (
  SELECT *,
    NTILE(5) OVER (ORDER BY recency   DESC) AS r_score,
    NTILE(5) OVER (ORDER BY frequency  ASC)  AS f_score,
    NTILE(5) OVER (ORDER BY monetary   ASC)  AS m_score
  FROM rfm_base
)
SELECT *,
  (r_score + f_score + m_score) AS total_rfm_score
FROM rfm_scored
ORDER BY total_rfm_score DESC;

💡 Key Learnings

  • RFM scoring is a simple but incredibly powerful way to prioritise customer outreach
  • Personalised campaigns to Champions and Loyal segments delivered 4x higher revenue than broad blasts
  • Suppressing Lost customers from paid retargeting significantly reduced wasted ad spend
  • Segment sizes shifted significantly month-over-month — monitoring dashboards are essential
